Tempeh Tacos

Ingredients

  • Tempeh
  • Tortilla shells (soft or hard)
  • Sweet Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Tomatoes
  • Avocado
  • Jalapeño or Habanero peppers
  • Fresh Garlic
  • Limes
  • Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Chili powder
  • Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Dried Cilantro
  • Dried Oregano
  • Salt
  • Olive or Avocado oil
  • Apple Cider Vinegar

Steps

  1. For 16oz of tempeh, mix the following spices:

    • 2 tbsp chili powder
    • 2 tsp cumin
    • 2 tsp paprika
    • 1 tsp dried cilantro
    • 1 tsp dried oregano
    • 1/2 tsp salt

  2. Finely chop 1 cup onion and 1/4 cup peppers
  3. Cube tempeh to roughly the size of your pinky-tip (does not have to be exact)
  4. Place pan on stove just below medium heat and add 2 tbsp oil
  5. Add peppers to heated pan and sauté until onions are translucent, stirring occasionally
  6. Add tempeh and mix with peppers/onions until tempeh appears 'wet'
  7. Add spice mixture and stir until tempeh is coated and there are no clumps of spice
  8. Reduce heat to low and allow to cook, stiring occasionally
  9. Chop tomatoes and onion to desired size to top
  10. Roughly chop cilantro
  11. Finely or coarsely grate cheese
  12. Slice or cube avocado
  13. Whip sour cream until it can be easily dolloped
  14. Slice limes, cutting across center to squeeze easily
  15. Periodically taste tempeh and spice accordingly if desired
  16. If using hard shells, place in onion at 200 degrees for 5-7 minutes or until golen brown
  17. If using soft shells, heat in a skillet until slightly toasty and pliable
  18. Assemble tacos in desired order (tempeh is often best on bottom) and serve
